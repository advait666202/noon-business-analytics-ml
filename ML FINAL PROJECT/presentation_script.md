Slide 7 – Title: The Segment Monetization Playbook

Building on that, let’s now look at slide seven, our Segment Monetization Playbook. This matrix defines exactly how we interact with the three clusters moving forward. For Segment A, our Loyalists exhibiting high spend and high frequency, the strategic action is to absolutely halt promotional discounting. Here, we actively push cart incentives and upsell aggressively to reliably increase their basket size. Finally, for Segment C, our At-Risk demographics showing low session frequency and high return rates, we must urgently trigger re-engagement by deploying automated win-back campaigns. By assigning distinct execution rules to every single user dynamically, we guarantee that our communications remain hyper-relevant and financially strategic at all times.
