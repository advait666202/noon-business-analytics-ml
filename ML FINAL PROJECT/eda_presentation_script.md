"Thank you. Before we dive into our advanced predictive models, it is crucial to understand the foundational data driving our intelligence—this brings us to our 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis. We approached this dashboard section through several clear lenses.

**Data Quality Summary**
Our very first step is verifying data integrity. On this tab, we audit our transactional records, instantly identifying missing values in our logs and ratings. By imputing these gaps using median values, we guarantee that our subsequent machine learning models are fundamentally unbiased and reliable.

**📊 Distributions**
Moving to distributions, we analyze the spread and shape of continuous variables. For instance, when looking at timestamps, we clearly see that purchase frequencies are heavily skewed, with massive spikes in perishable goods ordered precisely on Thursday evenings in anticipation of weekend demand. 

**📋 Category Counts**
Under the Category Counts tab, we examine categorical breakdowns. This reveals a distinct bimodal distribution in user engagement, highlighting a core group of high-frequency loyalists who drive top-line revenue, contrasted sharply against a long tail of dormant, one-time shoppers across various item segments.

**🔥 Correlation Heatmap**
Next, we use our Correlation Heatmap to instantly visualize how different features interact on a macro scale. This mathematical matrix allowed us to pinpoint critical relationships—such as how support ticket volume strongly correlates with lower order frequency, acting as a clear leading indicator for customer churn.

**🔀 Bivariate**
Finally, our Bivariate analysis explores targeted two-variable relationships in depth. For example, plotting order volume against location data isolates specific delivery zones suffering from higher logistical delays, allowing us to map operational bottlenecks directly to customer dissatisfaction.

Ultimately, this Exploratory Data Analysis didn't just clean our dataset; it fundamentally dictated our strategy. It informed the exact mathematical features we selected for predicting churn, clustering users, and forecasting demand. 

I'll now hand it over to discuss the first of our predictive solutions."
